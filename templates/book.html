Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my doctor's appointment?

+

Please bring:

  • Valid ID proof
  • Previous medical records
  • List of current medications
  • Insurance card (if applicable)
  • List of questions or concerns

How do I schedule an appointment?

+

Click on any doctor's card to schedule an appointment through their Calendly link. You'll be able to select a convenient time slot from their available schedule.

What are the consultation charges?

+

Consultation charges vary by specialist. General physician consultations start from ₹500, while specialist consultations may range from ₹800-₹1500.

How long is each consultation session?

+

Each consultation typically lasts 15-30 minutes, depending on the complexity of the case and the doctor's assessment.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

+

Yes, you can reschedule your appointment through the same Calendly link at least 24 hours before your scheduled time.
